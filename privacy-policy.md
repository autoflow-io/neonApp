# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-01-01

This application ("neonApp") uses TikTok for Developers APIs to allow users to upload and publish video content to their own TikTok accounts.

## Information We Collect
We do not collect or store personal user data.
Authentication is handled directly by TikTok via OAuth 2.0.

## Use of TikTok Data
The app accesses the following TikTok data only with explicit user consent:
- Basic account information (user.info.basic)
- Video upload and publishing permissions

This data is used solely to upload and publish content initiated by the user.

## Data Storage
Access tokens are stored securely and are never shared with third parties.

## User Control
Users may revoke access at any time from their TikTok account settings.
