3. OAuth 2.0 Integration

NeonApp uses OAuth 2.0 authentication protocols to enable secure access to your accounts with third-party providers while protecting your credentials.

3.1 How OAuth 2.0 Works in NeonApp

  • Authentication Flow: When you choose to log in or connect using OAuth 2.0 (Google, Microsoft, Facebook, TikTok, etc.), you are redirected to the respective provider's authentication server.
  • Authorization Scope: You explicitly authorize NeonApp to access specific permissions or scopes (e.g., read profile information, access content, etc.) as defined by each provider.
  • Token Exchange: Upon successful authentication, the provider issues an access token that NeonApp uses to authenticate your requests on your behalf.
  • No Password Storage: Your passwords for OAuth providers are never shared with or stored by NeonApp.

3.2 Supported OAuth 2.0 Providers

Provider Purpose Data Access Scope Privacy Link
Google Authentication & Profile Access Email, name, profile picture, basic account info Google Privacy Policy
TikTok Account Connection & Content Access User info, video list, engagement metrics TikTok Privacy Policy
Microsoft Authentication & Account Integration Email, profile, calendar data (if authorized) Microsoft Privacy
Facebook Authentication & Social Features Email, public profile, friend list (if authorized) Facebook Privacy Policy

3.3 OAuth Token Management

  • Token Storage: OAuth access and refresh tokens are encrypted and stored securely on our servers.
  • Token Expiration: Tokens are set to expire according to each provider's specifications.
  • Token Revocation: You can revoke NeonApp's access at any time through your OAuth provider's account settings or through NeonApp's settings page.
  • Token Refresh: When tokens expire, we automatically request new tokens using refresh tokens (when available) without requiring additional user authentication.

3.4 Data Sharing via OAuth

When you connect your accounts via OAuth 2.0, the following data flows may occur:

  • Provider → NeonApp: Your authorized profile information and access tokens
  • NeonApp → Provider: Actions you perform (e.g., posting, liking) if you authorize such actions
  • NeonApp does NOT: Share your information with other users or third parties through OAuth connections without explicit consent

5. TikTok Compliance & Data Handling

NeonApp integrates with TikTok's platform and is committed to full compliance with TikTok's Data Processing Addendum, Developer Terms of Service, and Community Guidelines.

5.1 TikTok OAuth Integration

  • Authentication Method: We use TikTok OAuth 2.0 for secure, user-authorized access to TikTok accounts.
  • Authorized Scopes: We only request scopes necessary for our service functionality (user info, video list, analytics, etc.).
  • Permission Transparency: During the OAuth flow, TikTok displays exactly what permissions we're requesting before you authorize.

5.2 TikTok Data We Access

With your explicit authorization, NeonApp may access:

  • TikTok user profile information (username, display name, avatar, bio)
  • Public and private video metadata (video ID, title, description, creation date)
  • Engagement metrics (view count, like count, comment count, share count)
  • Video performance data and trending insights
  • Follower/following information (as permitted by TikTok)
  • Comment and message data (if authorized for specific features)

5.3 How We Handle TikTok Data

  • Limited Processing: We process TikTok data only for the specific purposes you authorize and communicated clearly to you.
  • No Resale: We do NOT sell, trade, or share your TikTok data with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • Secure Storage: All TikTok data is encrypted and stored on secure servers with access controls.
  • User Control: You can revoke NeonApp's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings or NeonApp's connected apps page.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect TikTok data necessary to provide our services; we do not request unnecessary permissions.

5.4 TikTok Brand & Guidelines Compliance

  • NeonApp does NOT impersonate TikTok or misrepresent the source of data
  • We prominently display TikTok's branding and credit TikTok as the data source in our application
  • We comply with TikTok's Intellectual Property policy and do not use TikTok content for unauthorized purposes
  • We do NOT scrape, cache, or mirror TikTok content without explicit authorization
  • We adhere to TikTok's rate limits and API usage policies
  • Our service does NOT enable downloading or redistribution of TikTok videos without proper authorization

5.5 TikTok Creator Fund & Monetization Compliance

If you're a TikTok Creator Fund member:

  • We do NOT claim ownership of your content or earnings
  • We do NOT interfere with your TikTok monetization, Creator Fund eligibility, or payment processing
  • Analytics we provide are supplementary to TikTok's native analytics
  • Your earnings remain solely between you and TikTok

5.6 TikTok Account Safety

  • We strongly recommend using TikTok's two-factor authentication (2FA) for account security
  • NeonApp never requests your TikTok password; all authentication is OAuth-based
  • You maintain full control and can revoke our access at any time
  • We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your TikTok token and data

6. Data Storage & Security

6.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.2+ encryption (HTTPS).
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data (passwords, tokens, PII) is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest.
  • Database Security: Databases are protected with strong access controls, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate need have access to personal data.
  • Authentication: All access to data systems requires multi-factor authentication.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Incident Response: We maintain an incident response plan and will notify users of security breaches as required by law.

6.2 Data Storage Location

  • Primary data is stored in US-based data centers compliant with SOC 2 Type II standards.
  • Backup data is stored in geographically diverse locations for disaster recovery.
  • EU users' data may be processed with appropriate data transfer mechanisms (Standard Contractual Clauses).
  • You can request information about your data's physical storage location.

6.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a confirmed data breach affecting your personal information:

  • We will notify you within 72 hours (as required by GDPR and similar regulations)
  • Notification will include details of the breach, affected data, and recommended actions
  • We will cooperate with relevant regulatory authorities
  • We will offer complimentary credit monitoring or identity protection services where applicable

7. Your Rights & Control Over Your Data

7.1 Data Subject Rights (GDPR & Similar Laws)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data (right to be forgotten), subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request we limit how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain processing activities, including marketing communications and profiling.
  • Right to Lodge Complaints: You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for any processing you previously authorized.

7.2 Account Controls

You can manage your data through your account settings:

  • Profile Management: Update, modify, or delete profile information anytime
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust who can see your profile, content, and data
  • Connected Apps: View and revoke third-party app connections (OAuth tokens)
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-in/out of marketing emails, notifications, and analytics
  • Data Export: Download your data in CSV or JSON format
  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and associated data

7.3 Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@neonapp.com with:

  • Your name and email address associated with your account
  • A clear description of your request
  • Any supporting documentation
  • Your jurisdiction and applicable privacy law

We will respond to your request within 30 days (or 45 days for complex requests).

9. Data Retention

9.1 Retention Schedule
